Sampling Protocol

Vendor Selection

We monitor a mixture of popular vendors as well as those selling more obscure products. Selection is partly random to avoid systematic bias.

Anonymous Purchase

Samples are purchased using standard consumer accounts and shipping addresses. No indication of testing or research purposes.

Sample Processing

Upon receipt, samples are immediately cataloged and prepared for comprehensive analytical testing using our full suite of instruments.

Analysis & Reporting

Complete NMR, LC-MS, and FTIR analysis with detailed reporting of findings, including any safety concerns or adulterants detected.

Quality Assurance

No Vendor Bias

We do NOT work with vendors. If vendors want their samples tested and published, we will NOT work with them to avoid any conflict of interest.

Representative Sampling

By purchasing as normal users, we avoid vendors sending their "better" substances that aren't representative of what regular customers receive.

Comprehensive Coverage

Our monitoring includes both popular vendors and obscure sources to provide a complete picture of the market landscape.

Scientific Rigor

All samples undergo the same rigorous analytical protocols using state-of-the-art equipment and validated methodologies.
