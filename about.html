Our Mission

To provide analytical solutions for harm reduction in the field of newly emerging chemical entities, working with users and organizations to create Spectral Libraries that provide researchers and organizations with the tools they need to save lives and advance science, free of charge.

🛡️

Harm Reduction

Providing accurate, timely information about substance composition to reduce harm and save lives in the recreational drug-using community.

🔬

Scientific Excellence

Employing state-of-the-art analytical chemistry techniques and maintaining the highest standards of scientific rigor in all our analyses.

🌍

Public Service

Operating as a public service initiative, providing free spectral libraries and affordable testing services to advance harm reduction globally.

Laboratory Equipment

State-of-the-art analytical instruments ensuring the highest quality results

NMR Spectrometer

Nanalysis Pro100MHz

High-resolution nuclear magnetic resonance spectrometer capable of running all common types of 1D and 2D experiments including 1H and 13C NMR.

Field Strength: 100 MHz
Probe: 5mm PABBO
Temperature Range: -20°C to +80°C
Experiments: 1D, 2D, DEPT, COSY
Structural Elucidation Quantification Purity Analysis

LC-MS System

Agilent Triple Quadrupole

High-performance liquid chromatography coupled with triple-quadrupole mass spectrometry for precise quantification and identification of compounds.

Mass Range: 10-1000 m/z
Ionization: ESI, APCI
Sensitivity: pg/mL level
Chromatography: UHPLC
Quantification Impurity Detection Metabolite ID

FTIR Spectrometers

Fourier-transform infrared spectrometers for rapid identification of functional groups and confirmation of molecular structures.

Resolution: 0.5 cm⁻¹
Detector: DTGS, MCT
Sampling: ATR, Transmission
Rapid Screening Functional Group ID Quality Control

Our Journey

From concept to reality - our mission to advance harm reduction through science

2022 - Foundation

Kykeon Analytics founded with the mission to provide accessible, high-quality analytical services for harm reduction purposes. Initial team of 3 scientists established in Catalonia.

Company Founded First Equipment

2023 - Laboratory Expansion

Expanded laboratory facilities with addition of LC-MS system and second FTIR spectrometer. Team grew to 6 members across multiple disciplines.

LC-MS Added Team Expansion

2024 - Spectral Libraries

Launched free spectral libraries for harm reduction organizations. Reached milestone of 1,000 samples analyzed and 50+ organizations served.

Libraries Launched 1000+ Samples

2025 - Market Monitoring

Established comprehensive market monitoring program to detect novel substances and adulterants. Launched real-time alert system for dangerous substances.

Market Monitoring Alert System
